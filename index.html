Daily Challenge

December 15, 2020 (Level 1-2): Using the pieces 1, 6, and 5, make a straight line (the corner numbers that are next to eachother add up to 180). Extra (Level 3): Can you complete the puzzle without making perpendicular lines? Can you do it with creating perpendicular lines?

Daily: There should be a red, purple, and yellow corner touching. Extra: The answer is yes to both questions. The corners might be red-purple-yellow for part one, and purple-red-yellow or red-yellow-purple for part two.

Level 3 Challenges

10. Place two 5 pieces on top of each other so that green corners and orange corners overlap, and the two shortest sides overlap. Are the two longest sides perpendicular?

10. Yes.



11. Using a 6 and two 7s, make a pair of parallel lines which are both perpendicular to a side of 6.

11. There are two solutions: Use the purple angles to make two lines perpendicular to one side of 6. They are parallel. Alternatively, face one tip of 6 towards you. Lie the longest side of both 7’s along the “left” and “right” sides of 6, with the red corners pointing away from you.
